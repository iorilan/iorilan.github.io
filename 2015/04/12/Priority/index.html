Software Engineering

Priority

working software over coding quality

  • People like to share his coding style, but first of all , make your stuff works , show me your application , then share with me how you did and your unit test.
  • keeping it simple is always over a so called “perfect design”, do not waste your time.
  • trust agile does not only means being sensitive to bad codes and attack it ,but also, more important is shut up make it works ,refactor and finish your test.

Team Efficiency over individual preference

  • you love angular ,knockout js, it is ok , but they may not make team working more effective.
  • stop framework war , but if you are noob it is understandable. please put your focus on the way how you structure your classes ,algorithm and how you design your test cases.
  • move as team ,you are responsible for others problem . you dislike the codes he write is fine ,but please show him in IDE how you think , stop talking.

Doing over Talking

  • before you start blame others code , shut up and open IDE, run unit test , rethink the design you did , review each line of your code .
